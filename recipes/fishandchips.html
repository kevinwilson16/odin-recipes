Fish and Chips

Description

Fish and chips is a hot dish consisting of fried fish in batter, served with chips.

The dish originated in England.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into strips
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 Â½ pounds cod fillets

Steps

  1. Place sliced potatoes in a medium bowl and cover with cold water.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper together in a separate medium bowl.
  3. Add milk and egg; stir until batter is smooth. Let stand for 20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large pot or electric skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  5. Drain and pat dry potatoes. Fry in hot oil until tender, about 5 minutes; drain on paper towels.
  6. Dredge cod in batter, one piece at a time, and place in hot oil.
  7. Fry fish in batches until golden brown on all sides; drain on paper towels.
  8. Fry potatoes again in hot oil until crisp, 1 to 2 minutes; drain on paper towels.
